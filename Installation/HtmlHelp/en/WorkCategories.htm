Work Categories

< Previous section  |  Table of Contents  |  Index  |  Next section >

When work orders are issued they can be assigned a work category. Examples of work categories might be:

Mechanical repair
Electrical repair
Safety inspection
Clean-up
Planned maintenance

For information on viewing work categories, see Viewing Work Categories. For information on creating and editing work categories, see Editing Work Categories. For information on printing work categories, see Printing Work Categories.
