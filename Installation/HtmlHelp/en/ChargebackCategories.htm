Chargeback Categories

< Previous section  |  Table of Contents  |  Index  |  Next section >

The Chargeback Categories table makes it possible to group chargebacks into classes. Here are some typical chargeback categories:

Repair
Renovation
Leasehold improvement

While you don't have to create any chargeback categories at all, they are useful for organizing reports and for getting detailed breakdowns on time and expenses. If you have licensed the Accounting package, chargeback categories are also important for associating expenses with the correct ledger account.

For more on chargebacks, see Chargebacks.

For information on viewing chargeback categories, see Viewing Chargeback Categories. For information on creating and editing chargeback categories, see Editing Chargeback Categories. For information on printing chargeback categories, see Printing Chargeback Categories.
