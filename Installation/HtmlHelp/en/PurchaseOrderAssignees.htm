Purchase Order Assignees

< Previous section  |  Table of Contents  |  Index  |  Next section >

The purchase order assignee list specifies people who can be assigned to purchase orders. Assigning someone to a purchase order means that person has some special interest in the purchase order; for more, see Assignments.

For information on viewing purchase order assignee records, see Viewing Purchase Order Assignee Records. For how to create and edit purchase order assignee records, see Editing Purchase Order Assignee Records. For printing purchase order assignee records, see Printing Purchase Order Assignee Records.
