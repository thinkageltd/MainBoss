MainBoss Diagnostic Messages

< Previous section  |  Table of Contents  |  Index  |  Next section >

MainBoss displays messages in a variety of situations. For example, if you attempt to save a record before you've filled in all the required fields, MainBoss opens a message window to explain the situation. Messages may also be displayed in the status line at the bottom of MainBoss windows.
