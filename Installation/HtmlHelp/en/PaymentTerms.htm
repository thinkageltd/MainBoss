Payment Terms

< Previous section  |  Table of Contents  |  Index  |  Next section >

MainBoss purchase orders can specify payment terms. The Payment Terms table lists all the payment terms that might be used by your organization, so that these terms may appear on purchase orders. Sample payment terms might include

Pay on receipt
C.O.D.
Net 30
Net 60
Net 90

Note: For more on creating purchase orders with MainBoss, see Purchase Orders.)

For information on viewing payment terms, see Viewing Payment Terms. For information on creating and editing payment terms, see Editing Payment Terms. For information on printing payment terms, see Printing Payment Terms.
