The MainBoss Overview Display

< Previous section  |  Table of Contents  |  Index  |  Next section >

When you start MainBoss (after you've chosen a maintenance organization, if necessary), you'll see the MainBoss Overview display. This gives you a quick snapshot of the most important aspects of your current work: work requests that are new or in progress, work orders that are overdue, and so on. In this way, you can see anything that might demand immediate attention.

The button in this window updates the numbers to reflect any recent changes made by other people.
