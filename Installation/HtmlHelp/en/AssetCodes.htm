Asset Codes

< Previous section  |  Table of Contents  |  Index  |  Next section >

The Asset Codes table lets you record any fixed asset balance sheet account numbers that are used to capitalize equipment in your organization's general ledger. This makes it possible to cross-reference your equipment assets with your general accounts.

For information on viewing asset codes, see Viewing Asset Codes. For information on creating and editing asset codes, see Editing Asset Codes. For information on printing asset codes, see Printing Asset Codes.
