Shipping Modes

< Previous section  |  Table of Contents  |  Index  |  Next section >

When you create a purchase order with MainBoss, it is often useful to specify how the goods should be shipped. The Shipping Modes table lists all the shipment methods that your organization might request, making it possible for these terms to appear on purchase orders. Here are some examples:

Standard freight
Overnight courier
Pick-up
Regular mail

Note: For more on creating purchase orders with MainBoss, see Purchase Orders.)

For information on viewing shipping modes, see Viewing Shipping Modes. For information on creating and editing shipping modes, see Editing Shipping Modes. For information on printing shipping modes, see Printing Shipping Modes.
