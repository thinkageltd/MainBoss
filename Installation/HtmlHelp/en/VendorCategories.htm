Vendor Categories

< Previous section  |  Table of Contents  |  Index  |  Next section >

The Vendor Categories table makes it possible to group vendors into classes. Here are some typical vendor categories:

Electrical contractor
Plumbing contractor
Stationery stores
Equipment vendor

While you don't have to create any vendor categories at all, they are useful for organizing reports and for getting detailed breakdowns on time and expenses.

For information on viewing vendor categories, see Viewing Vendor Categories. For information on creating and editing vendor categories, see Editing Vendor Categories. For information on printing vendor categories, see Printing Vendor Categories.
