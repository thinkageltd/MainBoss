Billable Requestors

< Previous section  |  Table of Contents  |  Index  |  Next section >

A billable requestor is a person who can request work and be billed for the job. For example, a property management company might bill tenants for certain types of requested work.

Before you create a billable requestor record for someone, you should create a record for that person in the contacts table.

For information on viewing billable requestors, see Viewing Billable Requestor Records. For information on creating and editing billable requestors, see Editing Billable Requestor Records. For information on printing billable requestors, see Printing Billable Requestor Records.
